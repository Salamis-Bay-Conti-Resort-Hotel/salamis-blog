The Best Places to Visit in Cyprus

Ready to pack your bags for a Mediterranean adventure? Cyprus is far more than just a dot on the map. It is a place where sunshine, history, and breathtaking coastal views blend into one unforgettable experience. With its warm climate throughout most of the year, you can visit almost anytime. Spring and autumn are especially pleasant, offering perfect temperatures and fewer crowds. If you are trying to decide where to go first, here is a quick look at the island’s true highlights.

Top Destinations to Visit in Cyprus

From hidden gems to famous landmarks, these spots are the perfect starting point:

  • The Ancient Harbor of Kyrenia
  • Bellapais Abbey
  • The Salamis Ruins in Famagusta
  • Othello Castle
  • Varosha (Ghost Town)
  • The Venetian Column
  • Selimiye Mosque
  • The Karpaz Peninsula
  • The Icon Museum
  • The Walled City of Nicosia

When looking for the best places to visit in Cyprus, these locations will truly make your vacation unforgettable. They reflect the island’s rich past and vibrant present.

Things You Should Know Before Planning a Vacation in Cyprus

Planning your trip is exciting, and knowing a few essentials makes it even smoother. Cyprus is easy to explore, but these simple tips will help you enjoy your journey more comfortably.

  • Transportation: You can reach the island by air or sea. Flying is the most common option.
  • Flights: Direct flights from many Turkish cities arrive at Ercan Airport in about 1.5–2 hours.
  • Ferries: Ferries from Mersin or Alanya reach Kyrenia Harbor.
  • Currency: Turkish Lira is widely used. Euro and British Pound are also accepted in many places.
  • Traffic: Driving is on the left side of the road.
  • Food: From fresh halloumi to traditional kebabs, every meal is a Mediterranean delight.

Girne: Tourist Paradise in Cyprus

Kyrenia, known locally as Girne, is often called the jewel of the island. Imagine a horseshoe-shaped harbor filled with colorful fishing boats and lively cafes. The sea breeze and aroma of fresh coffee instantly create a holiday mood.

Kyrenia Castle overlooks the harbor and houses a shipwreck thousands of years old, making it one of the most fascinating attractions on the island.

Be Inspired by the Bellapais Abbey

Just a short drive from Kyrenia lies the peaceful village of Bellapais. Its famous abbey is a masterpiece of Gothic architecture dating back to the 13th century. Known as the “Abbey of Peace,” it offers breathtaking mountain views and serene surroundings.

A Journey Through History in Famagusta

Famagusta is like an open-air museum where medieval architecture meets modern coastal life. Walk along Venetian walls, explore Gothic cathedrals, and discover centuries of layered history.

A Cultural and Artistic Journey in Nicosia

As the last divided capital in the world, Nicosia offers a fascinating mix of cultures. Wander through narrow streets filled with artisan workshops and traditional markets. Büyük Han is one of the best places to experience the island’s cultural heritage.

Altınkum Beach: A True Natural Escape

Located on the Karpaz Peninsula, Altınkum Beach offers untouched golden sands and complete tranquility. It is widely considered the most peaceful beach on the island.

Notable Historical Sites for History Lovers

Cyprus has been home to countless civilizations, each leaving behind remarkable traces. From ancient settlements to Roman ruins, history lovers will find endless discoveries.

Salamis Ancient City

Located near Famagusta, Salamis was once the capital of Cyprus. Walk through its massive theater, explore the gymnasium, and admire marble columns that still stand proudly today. Its seaside location makes it one of the most unique historical sites in the Mediterranean.

Places Enchanting with Natural Beauty

Cyprus offers dramatic landscapes ranging from hidden coves to pine-covered mountains. These natural wonders are among the island’s most memorable attractions.

Beşparmak Mountains and St. Hilarion Castle

Rising high above the coastline, St. Hilarion Castle is one of the Mediterranean’s most spectacular fortresses. Its panoramic views and fairy-tale atmosphere make it a favorite for photographers and hikers alike.

Recommended Routes for Your Cyprus Trip

To make the most of your time, combine beaches, history, and scenic towns in one itinerary.

  • 1–2 Days: Kyrenia Focus — Harbor, castle, Bellapais, sunset dining.
  • 1 Day: Famagusta & Salamis — Medieval streets and ancient ruins.
  • 1 Day: Karpaz Escape — Golden Beach and untouched nature.
