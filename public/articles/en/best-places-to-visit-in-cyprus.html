The Best Places to Visit in Cyprus

Ready to pack your bags for a Mediterranean adventure? Cyprus is far more than just a dot on the map; it is a place where the sun always shines, and history is woven into every coastal view. If you are trying to decide where to go first, here is a quick look at the island's true highlights. From hidden gems to famous landmarks, these spots are the perfect starting point:

  • The Ancient Harbor of Kyrenia: A picturesque spot perfect for an evening stroll.
  • The Salamis Ruins in Famagusta: Where history comes alive right next to the sea.
  • The Ghost Town of Varosha: A hauntingly beautiful and unique experience.
  • Bellapais Abbey: A peaceful retreat with breathtaking mountain views.
  • Altınkum Beach: The most untouched golden sands you will ever see.
  • St. Hilarion Castle: Feel like you are in a fairy tale high above the clouds.
  • The Walled City of Nicosia: A cultural hub where the past and present collide.

When looking for the best places to visit in Cyprus, these spots are the ones that will truly make your vacation photos pop and your heart race with excitement.

Things You Should Know Before Planning a Vacation in Cyprus

Planning your trip is exciting, and knowing a few tips makes it even better. While deciding on places to visit in Cyprus, you will notice each region has its own energy. For those seeking places to visit on Cyprus's eastern coast, expect stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters.

Remember that traffic flows on the left side here. Most importantly, get ready for a feast! From fresh halloumi to local kebabs, every meal is a joy. Your Mediterranean adventure starts now!

Girne: Tourist Paradise in Cyprus

Kyrenia, or Girne, is often called the jewel of the island, and for good reason. Imagine a horseshoe-shaped harbor filled with colorful fishing boats and lined with buzzing cafes. As you walk through the harbor, the sea breeze and the smell of fresh coffee will instantly put you in a holiday mood.

When it comes to Cyprus places to visit, Girne is usually at the top of everyone’s list. The Kyrenia Castle stands tall over the harbor, housing a shipwreck that is thousands of years old.

Be Inspired by the Bellapais Abbey

Just a short drive up into the mountains from Girne, you will find the village of Bellapais. It is home to the stunning Bellapais Abbey, a 13th-century masterpiece of Gothic architecture. The name itself means "The Abbey of Peace," and you will understand why the moment you step inside. This is truly one of Cyprus's best places to visit if you need a moment of calm.

A Journey Through History in Famagusta

Famagusta (Gazimağusa) is where profound history meets a laid-back coastal lifestyle. This city is like an open-air museum. You can walk along the massive Venetian walls that still surround the old city or visit the Lala Mustafa Pasha Mosque, which was originally a Gothic cathedral.

Searching for Cyprus places to visit often leads people here because the combination of medieval ruins and modern life is so unique. If you are exploring the places to visit on Cyprus's eastern coast, Famagusta is your main hub.

A Cultural and Artistic Journey in Nicosia

Walking through Nicosia feels like stepping between two different worlds. As the last divided capital on earth, the city offers a fascinating blend of old and new. Once you pass through the ancient stone walls, you find yourself in a labyrinth of narrow paths where local craftsmen work alongside modern shops.

The Büyük Han is a must-see for anyone wanting to feel the island’s history. If you are looking for the best places to visit in Cyprus to really get a sense of the culture, this is it.

Altınkum Beach: A True Natural Escape

For those who prefer silence and untouched scenery, the Karpaz Peninsula is a mandatory stop. This is where Altınkum Beach, often called Golden Beach, stretches out under the sun. Many believe Aphrodite was born from the waves here, and the sparkling coastline makes that easy to imagine. Out of all the best places to visit on Cyprus, this is the most peaceful.

Notable Historical Sites for History Lovers

Cyprus has been home to many civilizations, and they all left their mark. For those who love digging into the past, the island is a treasure chest. From Neolithic settlements to Roman villas with intricate mosaics, you will never run out of things to explore. Also, some of the best places to visit in Cyprus for history buffs include the Soli Ancient City and the Vouni Palace.

Salamis Ancient City

Now, if you want to see the grandest of them all, you must visit the Salamis Ancient City. Located just north of Famagusta, this was once the capital of Cyprus. You can walk through the ancient gymnasium, sit in the massive theater that once held 15,000 people, and marvel at the marble columns that still stand tall against the blue sky. Among all Cyprus places to visit, Salamis is special because it is right on the coast.

Places Enchanting with Natural Beauty

Cyprus offers a raw landscape that changes from hidden bays to pine-covered mountains in a matter of miles. The contrast between the rugged cliffs and the clear Mediterranean water defines the island's character. If you are planning an itinerary, these scenic spots are definitely among Cyprus's best places to visit.

Beşparmak Mountains and St. Hilarion Castle

Sitting high above the coast, St. Hilarion Castle is one of the most impressive fortresses in the Mediterranean. Walking through its stone towers and steep staircases gives you a real sense of history. It remains one of the essential places to visit in Cyprus for anyone interested in architecture or photography.

Recommended Routes for Your Cyprus Trip

To make the most of your time, we recommend starting your day early. You could begin with a morning swim at a sandy beach in Famagusta, spend the afternoon exploring the Salamis ruins, and finish with a sunset dinner in Kyrenia. The best places to visit in Cyprus are often close to each other, so you do not need to spend hours in a car.
